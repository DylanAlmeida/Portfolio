Past Violent Criminals are everywhere and you can never be too careful in today's society. This app will help citizens sense the danger around them and allow people to know who is around them without being discrimatory towards the past offenders.

My app is targeted towards everyday average citizens and tracks violent criminals in their area and notifies them to be careful of them and lets them know the nature of the offender's crimes.

The app will be connected to The Federal Criminal Database and would relay information to citizens. There would be trackers within every offender's phone and if the offender is near you the app would send you a notification allowing you to know where you could be in danger.
